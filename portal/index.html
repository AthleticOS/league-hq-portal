Frequently Asked Questions
📅 Games & Scoring
How do I report a final score?

Go to the 'Games' tab, enter the Home and Away scores, select 'Completed' from the status dropdown, and tap 'Update Match'.

What does "Pending Verification" mean?

To ensure ledger accuracy, when you submit a score, your opponent must log in and hit 'Confirm'. Once confirmed, the match locks as Completed.

How do I respond to a reschedule request?

If a game is postponed and new dates are proposed, they will appear under the game card. You can Accept, Decline, or wait for your opponent to vote.

👥 Teams & Rosters
How do I register a new team?

Navigate to the 'Teams' tab, select the division you wish to compete in, and tap 'Register Team'. The division will then activate in your Rosters tab dropdown list.

Why can't I edit a player's Date of Birth?

To maintain league age-eligibility compliance, player birthdays are permanently locked upon submission. Contact your League Director to request an official change.

How do I update a player's jersey number?

Go to the Rosters tab, select the team, change the number directly in the player's card, and tap 'Save Changes'.

🏟️ Availability & Facilities
What is "Travel Only" vs "Not Available"?

"Not Available" means your team cannot play at all (e.g., a school holiday). "Travel Only" means your physical facility is booked, but your team is still available to play an away game.

How do I remove a blackout date I added by mistake?

Scroll to the 'Logged Exceptions Status' at the bottom of the Availability tab and click the red trash can icon next to the entry to permanently delete it.
